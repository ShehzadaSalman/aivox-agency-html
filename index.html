What It Handles

Common plumbing calls AIVox handles

  • Leaks (under sink, pipe, valve, outdoor spigot)
  • Clogged drains (kitchen, bath, main line concerns)
  • Toilet will not flush / overflow
  • Water heater issues (no hot water, leaking)
  • Low water pressure
  • Sewer smell / backup concerns
  • Install / replacement estimates (faucet, toilet, water heater)
  • Service area and "how soon can you come" questions
Emergency and safety: If the caller mentions gas smell, a dangerous situation, or a true emergency, AIVox advises them to contact local emergency services and can attempt to connect them to your on-call number immediately.

Frequently Asked Questions

Plumbing FAQ

Can AIVox answer after hours only?
+

Yes. Set it to answer only after-hours, weekends, or holidays, or use it as overflow when you miss a call during the day.

Will it transfer emergencies to me?
+

Yes. You can configure an urgent path that offers to connect the caller to your on-call number.

Will it give prices over the phone?
+

Only if you want it to. Most plumbers prefer to gather details, give a range, and confirm after inspection. AIVox can follow your preference.

Do I need a new phone number?
+

No. You can keep your existing number and route calls to AIVox based on your rules (after-hours, overflow, or always).

What if it cannot understand the caller?
+

AIVox can fall back to capture the message and details, or transfer to you/on-call depending on your setup.

Can it capture service area / ZIP filtering?
+

Yes. If a caller is outside your service area, AIVox can politely decline and still capture a lead if you want to refer out.

How fast can I get set up?
+

Typically within 24-48 hours, depending on how quickly you provide your business hours, service area, and call preferences.
